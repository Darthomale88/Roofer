Common Repairs

What We Fix

Active Leaks

Water stains on ceilings, attic moisture, or active drips. We trace the source, not just the symptom.

Missing or Damaged Shingles

Wind-lifted or cracked shingles replaced to match your existing roof as closely as possible.

Flashing Repair

Chimney, skylight, and vent flashing are the most common leak points on an otherwise healthy roof.

Valley & Ridge Repair

Where two roof planes meet is where water problems start. We rebuild valleys correctly, not just patch them.

Soft Spots & Deck Repair

Localized decking replacement where water has compromised the wood underneath.

Emergency Tarping

Storm damage or a sudden leak? We can tarp the same day to stop further damage while we schedule the repair.

Honest Assessment

Repair vs. Replace, Explained Plainly

If your roof is under 15 years old with isolated damage, a repair is usually the right call. If you're seeing widespread granule loss, multiple leak points, or the roof is past its expected lifespan, we'll tell you that too, along with why.
