Our Story

A Local Roofing Company, Built the Slow Way

Summit Ridge Roofing started with one truck and a commitment to show up when we said we would. Fourteen years later, that's still the whole business model.

How We Started

Founded in Indianapolis, 2012

Summit Ridge Roofing was founded on a simple frustration: too many homeowners were getting rushed estimates from crews they'd never see again. We built our company around the opposite idea, small enough that the owner still writes estimates, big enough to handle a full replacement in one to two days.

We've since grown to a full crew serving Indianapolis and the surrounding Marion County suburbs, but we've kept the same approach: inspect thoroughly, quote honestly, and clean up like we live there.
